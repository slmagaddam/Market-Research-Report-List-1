<p><p>The condensate tanks market includes two primary types: vertical and horizontal tanks. Horizontal tanks, on the other hand, provide greater stability and are easier to load and unload. They often have larger capacities suited for bulk storage. Both types are essential in managing condensate in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p></p>
