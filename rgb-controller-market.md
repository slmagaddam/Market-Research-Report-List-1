<p><p>The RGB controller market encompasses various types of control systems, primarily categorized into Radio Remote Control and Infrared Remote Control. Radio Remote Control operates on radio frequencies, allowing for longer ranges and the ability to control multiple devices without a direct line of sight. In contrast, Infrared Remote Control relies on light signals, necessitating a clear line of sight to function effectively, but offering a simpler and often more cost-effective solution. Both cater to diverse user needs in lighting and display applications.</p></p>
