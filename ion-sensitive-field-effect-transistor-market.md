<p><strong>The Ion Sensitive Field Effect Transistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>JFET Type</li><li>MOS Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ion Sensitive Field Effect Transistor (ISFET) market is primarily categorized into JFET and MOS types. JFET-type ISFETs utilize a junction field effect transistor structure, providing high sensitivity and fast response, making them suitable for applications like pH sensing. In contrast, MOS-type ISFETs leverage metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering scalability and integration with digital circuits, which is beneficial for advanced sensing applications. Both types serve various industries, including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety, addressing specific analytical needs.</p></p>
