<p><p>The cryogenic globe valves market comprises three main types: butt welding, flange, and thread types. Butt welding valves provide seamless connections for high-pressure applications, ensuring minimal leakage. Flange types, designed for ease of installation and maintenance, feature flat surfaces that allow mounting between two flanges. Thread types utilize threaded connections for easier assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for lower-pressure applications. Each type serves specific needs in industries dealing with cryogenic fluids, ensuring safety and efficiency in operations.</p></p>
