<p><p>Fresh air systems can be categorized into pipe type and non-pipe type markets. Pipe type systems involve ductwork that transports fresh air from outside to various indoor spaces, ensuring controlled airflow and ventilation. In contrast, non-pipe type systems rely on natural ventilation or mechanical means like fans to bring in fresh air without extensive ducting. Each market type offers different installation, maintenance, and operational characteristics, catering to diverse needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings for optimal air quality and comfort.</p></p>
