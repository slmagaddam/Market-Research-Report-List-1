<p><strong>The Quad Flat No-leads (QFN) Package Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cavity QFN</li><li>Plastic-moulded QFN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quad Flat No-leads (QFN) package market is primarily divided into two types: air-cavity QFN and plastic-molded QFN. Air-cavity QFN features a cavity under the die, enhancing thermal performance and enabling better heat dissipation, making it suitable for high-power applications. On the other hand, plastic-molded QFN involves encapsulating the die in a plastic material, offering protection and cost-effectiveness, ideal for a wide range of consumer electronics. Both types serve essential roles in modern electronic packaging solutions.</p></p>
