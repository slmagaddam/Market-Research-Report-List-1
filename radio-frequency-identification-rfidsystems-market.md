<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems are categorized into two main types: passive and active RFID systems. Passive RFID systems utilize tags that rely on the electromagnetic field generated by a reader for power, making them cost-effective and ideal for tracking inventory and assets. In contrast, active RFID systems contain their own power source, allowing for greater range and real-time tracking capabilities, suitable for applications like fleet management and high-value asset tracking. Both systems enhance inventory management and supply chain efficiency.</p></p>
