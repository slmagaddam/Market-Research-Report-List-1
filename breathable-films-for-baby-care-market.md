In contrast, PP films offer superior breathability and comfort, enhancing skin protection and reducing irritation. Both types are designed to provide essential moisture management, ensuring a dry and comfortable environment for infants, while simultaneously allowing for airflow to maintain skin health. Together, they play a crucial role in baby care products.</p></p>
