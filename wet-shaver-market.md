<p><p>The wet shaver market can be categorized into three main types: manual shavers, rotary shavers, and reciprocating shavers. Manual shavers typically feature straight blades for precise control, ideal for traditional wet shaving. Rotary shavers use circular blade systems, offering a smooth shave by adapting to facial contours, making them popular for their convenience. Reciprocating shavers employ oscillating blades that move back and forth, providing a close shave and thriving in the electric segment, appealing to those seeking efficiency and speed.</p></p>
