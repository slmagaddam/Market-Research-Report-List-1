<p><ul><li>Icing</li><li>(Half) Film Coat</li><li>Enteric Coat</li><li>Slow Release Garments</li><li>Controlled Release Garments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet coatings enhance drug delivery, appearance, and stability. **Icing coatings** provide a smooth, glossy finish, improving aesthetics and swallowability. **(Half) Film coats** are thin polymer layers offering taste masking and protection. **Enteric coats** resist stomach acid, releasing the drug in the intestines. **Slow-release garments** gradually release the active ingredient over time, maintaining therapeutic levels. **Controlled-release garments** precisely regulate drug release, optimizing efficacy and reducing dosing frequency. Each type caters to specific pharmaceutical needs, ensuring better patient outcomes and product performance.</p></p>
