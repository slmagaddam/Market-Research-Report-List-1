<p><p>Coast Surveillance Radar systems are categorized primarily into X-Band and S-Band radar types, along with some other variants. </p><p>X-Band radar operates at higher frequencies, offering higher resolution and precision for detecting smaller objects, making it ideal for close-range surveillance and tracking. </p><p>In contrast, S-Band radar has longer wavelengths, providing better performance in adverse weather conditions and covering larger areas. </p><p>Other types may include L-Band and UHF radars, each designed for specific operational needs, enhancing overall maritime safety and security.</p></p>
