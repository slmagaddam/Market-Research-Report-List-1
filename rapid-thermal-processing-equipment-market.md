<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) equipment market encompasses two primary types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP utilizes high-intensity lamps to deliver rapid and uniform heating across substrates, making it suitable for various semiconductor and photovoltaic applications. In contrast, laser-based RTP employs focused laser beams for precise heating, allowing for selective processing and minimizing thermal impact on surrounding materials. Both types are essential for enhancing semiconductor fabrication efficiency and improving the performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
