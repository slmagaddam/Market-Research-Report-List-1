<p><p>The CO2 gas sensor market comprises several types, each using distinct technologies for detection. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 sensors measure gas concentration by detecting infrared light absorption, offering high accuracy and stability. Catalytic CO2 sensors rely on chemical reactions to sense gas presence, typically in industrial applications. Thermal conductivity CO2 sensors gauge changes in thermal conductivity of air due to CO2 presence, making them suitable for high concentrations. These technologies cater to various industries, enhancing environmental monitoring and safety.</p></p>
