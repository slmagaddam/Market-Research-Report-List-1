<p><p>The PP film market comprises three main types: BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film, CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film, and others. BOPP film is known for its clarity, strength, and moisture resistance, making it ideal for packaging and labeling. CPP film, on the other hand, offers excellent sealability and is commonly used for flexible packaging applications. Other PP films include specialty films with varied properties for specific uses, catering to diverse industries like food packaging, textiles, and medical supplies.</p></p>
