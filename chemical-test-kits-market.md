<p><p>The chemical test kits market is generally categorized into two types: single-parameter and multi-parameter kits. Single-parameter kits are designed to test one specific chemical or substance, offering high sensitivity and precision for targeted analysis. In contrast, multi-parameter kits can test multiple substances simultaneously, providing a broader overview of sample composition, which is useful for more comprehensive assessments. Both types are essential for various applications, including environmental monitoring, water quality testing, and industrial processes, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
